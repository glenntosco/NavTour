## FEATURES NOBODY HAS — NavTour.cloud Exclusives

These are features no competitor currently offers. Build these first for maximum differentiation:

### 1. AI Demo Agent (Conversational)
Storylane has "Lily" and Supademo has "AI Demo Agents" but neither is a true conversational agent. Build a **real-time AI agent** that:
- Answers prospect questions during the demo via chat
- Dynamically navigates to relevant demo sections based on questions
- Qualifies leads through natural conversation
- Hands off to a human rep when ready
- Works in 25+ languages

### 2. Real-Time Co-Browsing
No competitor offers this. Let a sales rep **join a prospect's demo session live**:
- See exactly where the prospect is in the demo
- Take control to guide them
- Draw/annotate in real-time
- Voice/video call overlay
- Handoff from AI agent to human seamlessly

### 3. Demo Builder from Figma/Design Files
Beyond Supademo's Figma plugin (which just imports screenshots), build a system that:
- Converts Figma prototypes directly into interactive demos
- Preserves Figma interactions and transitions
- Auto-generates flows from Figma frames
- Two-way sync — update Figma, demo updates automatically

### 4. Demo Analytics with Revenue Attribution
Go beyond pipeline "influence" (Navattic) to **direct revenue attribution**:
- Track: demo viewed → opportunity created → deal closed → revenue
- Show actual $ per demo, not just "influenced"
- ROI calculator built into analytics
- Benchmark against industry averages

### 5. White-Label Reseller Platform
No competitor offers this. Let agencies and SaaS companies:
- Resell NavTour under their own brand
- Custom domains, logos, colors per client
- Client management dashboard
- Revenue share model

### 6. Interactive Demo Templates Library
Nobody has a public template marketplace:
- Pre-built demo templates by industry (SaaS, FinTech, Healthcare, etc.)
- One-click clone and customize
- Community-contributed templates
- "Demo of the week" showcase

### 7. Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)
No competitor mentions accessibility compliance:
- Screen reader support
- Keyboard-only navigation
- High contrast mode
- Focus indicators
- ARIA labels on all interactive elements
- Accessibility audit report per demo

### 8. Multi-Product Demo Journeys
Connect demos across products:
- Cross-product storytelling
- Unified analytics across product demos
- Conditional routing based on interest
- Single hub for entire product suite

### 9. Demo Performance Scoring
AI-powered quality scoring:
- Score every demo on: clarity, engagement potential, CTA effectiveness
- Compare against top-performing demos in the industry
- Specific improvement suggestions
- A/B test recommendations

### 10. Embeddable Demo Widget (No iFrame)
Web component that embeds natively:
- No iframe — renders as part of the page
- SEO-friendly content
- Responsive by default
- Lazy-loaded for performance
- Shadow DOM isolation

## COMPETITOR WEAKNESSES TO EXPLOIT

### Navattic Weaknesses
- Steep learning curve for new users
- No screenshot/video-first demos (HTML-focused)
- No Figma plugin or desktop app
- No zoom/pan capability
- No autoplay, background music, closed captions
- No export as PDF/PNG (SOP format)
- Expensive ($500/mo for first real plan)
- No free trial on paid plans
- Limited AI compared to Storylane

### Supademo Weaknesses
- No HTML capture until Growth ($350/mo)
- No sandbox until Growth
- No beacon/pulsing elements
- No scroll lock/sync or element tethering
- No presenter mode
- No pipeline impact analytics
- No CRM-level integrations (Pardot, Outreach, Gong)
- No offline demos
- No sales enablement extension

### Storylane Weaknesses
- No webcam/microphone recording
- No desktop app capture
- No capture compare (before/after)
- No recapture workflow
- No auto-navigate capture mode
- No background music or closed captions
- No custom font upload
- No CTA banner in themes
- Account reveal limited (250/mo on $40 plan)
- Sandbox is Enterprise-only
- No in-app commenting

### Reprise Weaknesses
- Extremely expensive (~$28K/yr median)
- 1-3 month setup time
- No free tier or self-serve
- No screenshot or video demo format
- No mobile swipe demos
- No AI voiceover or voice cloning
- No form builder
- No playbook automation
